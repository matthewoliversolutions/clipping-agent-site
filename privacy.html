Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026  ·  Clipping Agent

1. Introduction

Clipping Agent ("we", "our", or "the Service") is an automated AI clipping tool that monitors live streams and publishes highlight clips to social media platforms on behalf of authorised users. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

We collect and process only the minimum data necessary to operate the Service:

  • TikTok access tokens — OAuth tokens issued by TikTok when you authorise the Service. Used solely to upload and publish video content on your behalf.
  • Stream metadata — URLs, timestamps, and clip identifiers generated during normal operation. Not shared with third parties.
  • Usage logs — Internal operational logs (errors, upload status). Not associated with personal identifiers and retained for a maximum of 30 days.

We do not collect your TikTok username, profile information, follower data, or any content from your TikTok account beyond what is required to complete an upload.

3. How We Use Your Data

Data is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with TikTok on your behalf using the Content Posting API.
  • Upload and publish video clips you have approved for posting.
  • Diagnose errors and maintain service reliability.

4. TikTok API Data

The Service uses the TikTok Content Posting API with the video.publish scope. We access this scope only to post content you have explicitly authorised. We do not use this access to read your feed, messages, followers, or any other account data.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except where required by law or to provide the core Service (e.g. transmitting video data to TikTok's API endpoints).

6. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are stored only as long as necessary to fulfil active upload requests. You can revoke access at any time via your TikTok account settings under "Manage app permissions". Upon revocation, stored tokens are deleted within 24 hours.

7. Security

Access tokens are stored encrypted at rest. We use HTTPS for all API communications. Access to stored credentials is restricted to the automated process that performs uploads.

8. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any data we hold about you at any time by contacting us. You also have the right to revoke the Service's access to your TikTok account at any time via TikTok's app permission settings.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, contact the operator via the contact details provided in the app.
